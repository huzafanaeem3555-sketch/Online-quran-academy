Our Courses

Every step of the journey, taught with care

Pick a path, or start with a free trial class and we will guide you to the right one.

Quran Reading with Tajweed

Learn correct pronunciation and the rules of Tajweed step by step, with personal correction every class.

Nazra Quran & Recitation

Build fluent, confident recitation from the Mus'haf with a teacher listening and guiding you line by line.

Hifz-ul-Quran (Memorization)

Structured memorization plan with daily revision — sabaq, sabqi, manzil — fully tracked by your teacher.

Tafseer-ul-Quran (Basic)

Understand the meaning behind the verses with simple, easy-to-follow explanations at your own level.

Islamic Duas & Wazaif

Daily duas, masnoon wazaif and their meanings — practical learning for everyday Muslim life.

Islamic Studies for Kids

Fun, age-appropriate lessons — basic beliefs, manners and stories of the Prophets for young learners.

Classes For Everyone
